ANNUITY
What is an annuity?
An annuity is a fixed annual allowance provided by an investment. An annuity can provide a guaranteed regular income for the rest of your life or for a specified number of years.
The amount of income provided through an annuity is generally determined at the time of purchase, and will depend on:

The amount of money deposited
The current interest rate
Your age
Your sex
The number of years for which the company promises to make payments
